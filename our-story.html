Burple is back and better than ever!

Our Origins

Burple was a drink mix that was packaged in an iconic accordion-like plastic bottle. There were two sizes available: lunch-box and full size. It was distributed by Sundale Beverages from Belmont, California and sold mainly in the Midwest.

Our Rebrand

Once upon a time, we were a powder-mixing drink with a unique bottle that kids around the world loved! Today, we have evolved into something even better. We are now a kids' yogurt brand that makes yoghurt that is creamy, scrumptious, and healthy for growing bodies. We kept our playful spirit and adopted a new iconic bottle shape!

Our Mission and Values

Burple encourages healthy habits through products made with real dairy, reduced sugar, and added probiotics to support growing bodies. The brand values creativity, wellness, and trust. By blending fun with nutrition, Burple aims to empower kids and give parents confidence in every sip.

Our Star Ingredients

Live Active Cultures

Helps support digestion

Burple highlights the power of live active cultures as a key part of its commitment to kids’ health. Each yogurt drink is made with carefully selected probiotics that help support digestion and overall gut health, making it easier for growing bodies to get the most out of their nutrition.

No Added Sugar

Naturally sweet with no extras

Burple puts a strong emphasis on having no added sugar, reflecting its commitment to healthier choices for kids. Instead of relying on extra sweeteners, Burple uses the natural sugars found in dairy and fruit to create a balanced and enjoyable taste.

Source of Protein

Helps build strong bodies

Burple emphasizes being a reliable source of protein to support kids’ growth and everyday energy needs. Made with quality dairy, each yogurt drink provides essential protein that helps build strong muscles and keeps kids feeling full and focused throughout the day.

Source of Fibre

Made with real fruit

Burple highlights the use of real fruit to bring natural flavour and added nutritional value to every yogurt drink. Made with carefully selected fruit purées, each serving delivers authentic taste without relying on artificial flavours or colours.
